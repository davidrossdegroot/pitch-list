## Summary
This initiative focuses on educating the community about waste management practices to reduce urban trash.

## Scope

- Workshops and events will be held in various neighborhoods to promote recycling and proper waste disposal.


## Data / Rationale

- Education is key to changing waste disposal behaviors.

- Community-led initiatives can foster ownership and responsibility.

- Aligns with city goals for sustainability.


## Risks & Mitigations

- Potential low turnout for events.

- Difficulty in measuring long-term behavior change.


## Success Metrics

- Number of participants in workshops.

- Pre- and post-event surveys on waste management knowledge.
